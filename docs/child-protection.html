Policy on Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation – Rate Master

Rate Master strictly prohibits any content that sexually exploits or endangers children.

Zero Tolerance

We have a zero-tolerance policy against child sexual abuse materials (CSAM). Any content of this nature will be immediately removed, and the users involved will be reported to the appropriate legal authorities.

Content Moderation

We actively monitor user-generated content and provide reporting tools for users to flag inappropriate content. Reports are reviewed promptly by our moderation team.

Reporting

If you encounter any suspicious or illegal activity, please contact us at: willialfred24@gmail.com.

Compliance

We comply with applicable laws and cooperate fully with child protection organizations and law enforcement, including reporting to organizations such as the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) where applicable.
