Account Deletion Request – Rate Master

If you have an account with Rate Master and wish to delete it, you can request the deletion by following the steps below.

How to Delete Your Account

To request the deletion of your account and associated personal data:

  • Send an email to willialfred24@gmail.com
  • Use the subject: "Account Deletion Request – Rate Master"
  • Include the email address used to register your account.

What Will Be Deleted

Upon confirmation, we will permanently delete the following:

  • Your user account and login credentials
  • Any ratings, comments, or reviews associated with your account
  • All profile information and preferences (if saved)

Data Retention

Some data may be retained for legal or security reasons (e.g., transaction logs) for a maximum of 1 month before permanent deletion.

If you have any questions, contact us at willialfred24@gmail.com.
